01

Patient Registration & Queue Management

👤 Primary Users: Front Desk
Workflow Explanation

When a patient arrives, the front desk creates a profile capturing demographics and history, generating a Unique Health ID (UHID) and a digital token. Returning patients are instantly verified via mobile number or a quick QR scan, entirely bypassing the long paper registration form.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Drastically reduces waiting room crowding, crucial for your 100+ daily OPD traffic.
  • Eliminates duplicate patient records and lost files.
  • Provides a structured, live numbered queue for doctors to view on their consultation screens.
02

Doctor's Consultation Dashboard

👤 Primary Users: Consulting Doctors
Workflow Explanation

Doctors log in to see their live queue. Clicking a patient opens their entire history. The doctor can type clinical notes, select diagnoses from an ICD-10 drop-down, and write e-prescriptions. With one click, lab tests are ordered and medicine lists are routed directly to the pharmacy.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Saves doctors from repeatedly writing manual paper prescriptions.
  • Instant access to past visit histories enables faster, more accurate diagnosis.
  • Creates a secure, digital Electronic Health Record (EHR) that cannot be lost or destroyed.
03

Centralized Billing & Cash Counter

👤 Primary Users: Billing Dept, Cashiers
Workflow Explanation

All charges from every department (Consultation fees, Ward rent, Pharmacy items, Lab tests) automatically flow into a single, centralized invoice. The cashier accepts payments, splits them if necessary (e.g., partial Cash, partial UPI), and generates a GST-compliant receipt.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Zero manual calculation errors during patient checkout.
  • Prevents massive revenue leakage by ensuring no consumed service or medicine goes unbilled.
  • Generates clear end-of-day financial reconciliation reports for management.
04

Staff Attendance & Role Security

👤 Primary Users: HR, Hospital Admin
Workflow Explanation

Staff log in via secure PIN or biometric. The system tracks shifts and duty allocations. Most importantly, it enforces strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). A receptionist only sees appointments; a nurse only sees vitals; a cashier only sees billing totals.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Ensures absolute data privacy and prevents staff from viewing sensitive financial data.
  • Automates staff attendance tracking for rapid, accurate payroll processing.
  • Maintains a strict audit log of exactly who modified which patient record and when.
05

Owner's Real-Time Analytics App

👤 Primary Users: Hospital Owner
Workflow Explanation

A dedicated, high-level dashboard accessible via the owner's mobile phone or laptop. It silently pulls live data from all operational departments to display today's total revenue, patient footfall, active bed occupancy, and pending TPA claims.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Complete transparency. Know exactly what is happening in the hospital from anywhere in the world.
  • Easily identify trends (e.g., which specific doctor or department brings in the most revenue).
  • Empowers fast, data-driven business decisions for hospital scaling.

Layer 2: Hospital Operations (A-La-Carte)

Department-specific tools. Select only the modules required for your specific operational layout.

06

IPD Bed & Ward Management

👤 Primary Users: Nurses, IPD Coordinators
Workflow Explanation

When admitted, a live visual map shows available beds. Nurses use this module to chart daily vitals (BP, Temp, SpO2), log medicine administration times, and record nursing notes. When ready for discharge, the system auto-compiles all data into a PDF discharge summary.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Perfect tracking of 26+ beds to maximize occupancy and prevent double-booking.
  • Ensures strict patient care protocols are followed exactly as prescribed by the doctor.
  • Speeds up the notoriously painful discharge process from hours to mere minutes.
07

Surgical Command Center (OT Tracker)

👤 Primary Users: Surgeons, OT Managers
Workflow Explanation

Crucial for handling 25+ weekly surgeries. A visual calendar allows conflict-free OT booking. Digital pre-op checklists (consent, fasting) are verified before entry. Post-surgery, the exact number of implants, sutures, and specialized drugs consumed are logged and auto-billed.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Absolutely zero scheduling clashes between busy surgeons and anaesthetists.
  • Prevents massive revenue loss by ensuring every high-value surgical consumable is billed.
  • Tracks precise OT utilization times to improve daily efficiency.
08

Smart Pharmacy & Medicine Manager

👤 Primary Users: Pharmacists
Workflow Explanation

The pharmacist receives the doctor's e-prescription directly on their screen. As medicines are dispensed, inventory is automatically deducted. The system tracks batch numbers, flags medicines nearing expiry, and auto-generates Purchase Orders (POs) for low stock.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Prevents stock-outs of fast-moving, high-revenue medicines.
  • Reduces financial loss from expired stock through predictive alerts.
  • Eliminates life-threatening dispensing errors caused by unreadable doctor handwriting.
09

Laboratory & Diagnostics Hub

👤 Primary Users: Pathologists, Lab Techs
Workflow Explanation

Lab orders flow digitally from the doctor. Techs generate barcodes for collected samples. Results are entered into the system; abnormal values are automatically highlighted in red. Once approved by the pathologist, a branded digital report is generated and attached to the EHR.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Speeds up report delivery to doctors, enabling faster, more accurate treatment.
  • Maintains strict quality control and tracks lab revenue separately from general OPD.
10

Hospital Asset & Store Inventory

👤 Primary Users: Store Managers, Admin
Workflow Explanation

Tracks everything *except* medicine. Manages syringes, cotton, bedsheets, medical machinery, and office supplies. It tracks internal departmental requests (Stock Indents), vendor purchase history, and equipment maintenance schedules.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Stops internal pilferage and wastage of costly hospital supplies.
  • Tracks exactly where hospital funds are being spent operationally.
  • Ensures expensive machinery is maintained on schedule, extending lifespan.
11

TPA, Insurance & Ayushman Bharat Desk

👤 Primary Users: Insurance Coordinators
Workflow Explanation

A dedicated workflow for cashless patients. Staff verify Ayushman Bharat eligibility, upload documents for pre-authorization, and track the status of insurance claims (Pending, Approved, Rejected). It strictly tracks the discrepancy between billed amounts and settled amounts.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Ensures hospital cash flow isn't choked due to missing insurance paperwork.
  • Provides clear reconciliation of TPA payments vs. internal hospital bills.
12

Ambulance Fleet Tracking

👤 Primary Users: Transport Admin
Workflow Explanation

Logs ambulance dispatch times, assigns drivers, and tracks trip details. The cost of the ambulance trip is automatically pushed to the patient's final IPD bill. Also maintains logs for ambulance maintenance and fuel costs.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Ensures ambulance trips are never "forgotten" during final discharge billing.
  • Optimizes driver utilization and vehicle upkeep.
13

Blood Bank Management

👤 Primary Users: Blood Bank Techs
Workflow Explanation

Maintains a registry of donors with screening results. Tracks the live inventory of different blood groups and components (Plasma, Platelets). Manages cross-matching logs and tracks blood issued for internal surgeries or sold to external hospitals.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Critical for surgical support; ensures you never run out of required blood types.
  • Maintains strict compliance and safety standards for blood storage.
14

Patient Diet & Kitchen Routing

👤 Primary Users: Dieticians, Kitchen Staff
Workflow Explanation

The doctor or dietician specifies a diet (e.g., Diabetic, Liquid, Low Sodium) in the IPD file. This order is routed directly to a kitchen display. Kitchen staff mark when the specific meal is prepared and delivered to the correct ward/bed.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Prevents dangerous dietary errors for critical patients.
  • Streamlines kitchen operations and significantly reduces food wastage.
15

Housekeeping & Sanitation

👤 Primary Users: Housekeeping Supervisors
Workflow Explanation

When a patient is discharged, the system instantly alerts housekeeping to clean the room. The bed remains marked "Dirty" in the system until the cleaning staff digitally signs off that sanitation is complete, only then making it available for new admission.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Accelerates bed turnaround time safely, increasing revenue.
  • Maintains strict hygiene accountability among cleaning staff.
16

CSSD (Sterile Tools Tracking)

👤 Primary Users: CSSD Techs, OT Managers
Workflow Explanation

Tracks surgical instruments through the Central Sterile Services Department. After an operation, tools are logged as "Contaminated." The system tracks them through washing, autoclaving, and packing, ensuring they are certified sterile before the next surgery.

Key Hospital Benefits
  • Prevents dangerous surgical site infections (SSI) and potential legal liabilities.
  • Ensures OT schedules aren't delayed due to missing or unsterilized equipment.
